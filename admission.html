Admission Process

Admissions start with the notification from Directorate of Technical education (DTE) generally after the announcement of SSLC/SSC board results.

Admission to first year are under:

  • Govt Quota: The admissions are through central counseling at the nodal centres as per the DTE procedures.
  • Management Quota: The procedure followed is as under
    1. Invitation of applications in the prescribed institutional format.
    2. Written test.
    3. Allotment of seat through counseling.
    4. Admission with prescribed fees.

Eligibility

Pass in SSLC/SSC of state govt or Central Govt boards with minimum marks requirements as prescribed by the DTE.
